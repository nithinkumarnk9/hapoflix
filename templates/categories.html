MOVIES

Movies are visual stories told through moving images and sound, designed to entertain, inform, or inspire.

COMEDY

Comedy is a genre of entertainment that uses humour to amuse and entertain audiences.

SONGS

Songs are musical compositions that combine melodies, lyrics, and rhythms to express emotions, tell stories, or convey messages.

WEB SERIES

A web series is a series of scripted or non-scripted videos released online, often in episodic form, typically designed for streaming platforms.

CARTOONS

Cartoons are animated visual content, often humorous, created for entertainment, typically targeting children or family audiences.

SPORTS

Sports are competitive physical activities or games that aim to improve skills, provide entertainment, and promote physical fitness.
